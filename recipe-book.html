Easy Air Fryer Pork Chops

  • Time: 25 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
grated Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup
paprika 1 teaspoon
garlic powder 1 teaspoon
kosher salt 1 teaspoon
dried parsley 1 teaspoon
ground black pepper 1/2 teaspoon
boneless pork chops 4(5 ounce)
extra virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons

Step 1: Preheat the air fryer to 380 degrees F (190 degrees C).

Step 2: Combine Parmesan cheese, paprika, garlic powder, salt, parsley, and pepper in a flat shallow dish; mix well.

Step 3: Coat each pork chop with olive oil. Dredge both sides of each chop in the Parmesan mixture and set on a plate.

Step 4: Place 2 chops in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes; flipping halfway through cook time. Transfer to a cutting board and let rest for 5 minutes. Repeat with remaining chops.

Step 5: Serve hot and enjoy!

Easy Korean Beef Bowl

  • Time: 25 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
lean ground beef 1 pound
garlic, crushed 5 cloves
freshly grated ginger 1 tablespoon
toasted sesame oil 2 teaspoons
reduced-sodium soy sauce 1/2 cup
light brown sugar 1/3 cup
crushed red pepper 1/4 teaspoon
green onions, chopped, divided 6 stalks
hot cooked brown rice 4 cups
toasted sesame seeds 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef and cook, stirring and crumbling into small pieces until browned, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain excess grease.

Step 2: Stir in garlic, ginger, and sesame oil and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes. Stir in soy sauce, brown sugar, and red pepper. Cook until beef absorbs some sauce, about 7 minutes. Add 1/2 of chopped green onions.

Step 3: Serve over hot cooked rice; garnish with sesame seeds and remaining green onions.

Honey Glazed Chicken

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Honey 1/4 cup
soy sauce 2 tablespoons
red pepper flakes 1/8 teaspoon
olive oil 1 1/2 tablespoons
chicken breast cut into bite-size pieces 2 halves

Step 1: Gather all ingredients

Step 2: Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.

Step 3: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.

Step 4: Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more

Step 5: Serve hot and enjoy!
